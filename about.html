Trained as an architect. Operating beyond it — and directing from there. Years of practice across Southern Europe and beyond — working at the scale of villas, master plans and cities, at studios that think across disciplines.

The work moves between architecture, spatial research, brand strategy and visual culture — directing each with the same clarity — not because the boundaries are unclear, but because they were never interesting to begin with.

Alongside architectural practice runs an independently directed line of work in video, photography, editorial and AI-assisted design — each project self-initiated, self-produced, self-edited. Shaped by exposure to Slavic, Mediterranean and Latin American contexts — each one leaving a different understanding of how people inhabit space and image.

Everything connects. Nothing is separate.

Currently available for art direction, spatial branding, and creative strategy roles in European studios.
