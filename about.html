Practice

Trained as an architect. Operating beyond it. Years of practice across Southern Europe and beyond — at the scale of villas, master plans and cities, at studios that think across disciplines.

The work moves between architecture, spatial research, brand strategy and visual culture — not because the boundaries are unclear, but because they were never interesting to begin with.

A parallel practice in video, photography, custom clothing and AI-assisted design. Shaped by Slavic, Mediterranean and Latin American contexts — each one a different understanding of how people inhabit space and image.

Everything connects. Nothing is separate.
